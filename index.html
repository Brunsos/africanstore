Who We Are

Your Home Away from Home

CCIO Afri-Carib Home Mart was built for Calgary's vibrant African and Caribbean community — a place to find the authentic ingredients that make a house feel like home. Whether you're from Nigeria, Ghana, Jamaica, Trinidad, or anywhere across the continent and islands, you'll find something familiar here.

Wide product selection — hundreds of African and Caribbean staples stocked regularly

Community-first approach — proudly serving Calgary's multicultural families

Convenient SW Calgary location — easy access at Belmont Village shopping centre

Friendly, knowledgeable staff — here to help you find exactly what you need
